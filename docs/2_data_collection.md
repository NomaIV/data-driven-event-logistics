# Data Collection Methodology

## Survey Design
**Platform:** Microsoft Forms  
**Distribution:** Company-wide email announcement  
**Duration:** Open for 7 days  
**Response Rate:** ~88% of workforce

## Survey Structure
### Section 1: Demographic Information
- Department/Team
- Shift pattern

### Section 2: Sports Preferences
- Multi-select from 7 sporting codes
- Ranking of preferred activities

### Section 3: Availability
- Preferred time slots
- Willingness to captain/organise

## Data Quality Measures
- Required fields for department selection
- Duplicate submission prevention
- Clear definitions of each sport's requirements
