# Project Overview: Unilever Sports Day Event

## Business Context
Unilever's manufacturing site, with over 1,000 employees, required an annual Sports Day event to promote wellness, team building, and employee engagement. Previous events suffered from low participation and logistical issues due to poor planning.

## Project Objectives
1. **Increase Participation:** Achieve >80% employee engagement
2. **Ensure Logistical Feasibility:** All activities must have minimum required participants
3. **Data-Driven Decisions:** Replace guesswork with quantitative analysis
4. **Create Reusable Framework:** Develop templates for future events

## Constraints & Challenges
- Fixed 6-hour time window
- Limited sports facilities (2 fields, 4 courts)
- Diverse workforce with varying interests
- Departmental representation requirements
